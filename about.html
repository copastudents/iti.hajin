About us:

Government Industrial Training Institute (ITI) Hajin, Bandipora,stands as premier institution of technical and vocational excellence in jammu and kashmir. Operating under the Directorate of skill Development, the institute plays a strategic role in developing- a highly skilled, adaptable, and industry-ready workforce aligned with both national priorities and global economic trends. Over time ,ITI has evolved beyond the conventional framework of vocational training to emerge as a catalyst for sustainable socio-economic transformation.In a region navigating its path toward stability and growth, the institute embodies resilience, opportunity,and the transformative power of skill-based education.

Vision and Mission

To establish a progressive ecosystem of Skill Development that fosters innovation , self-reliance, and inclusive growth , enabling individuals to thrive in a competitive global environment.

To deliver industry- aligned, competency-based training that bridges the gap between workforce demand and skill supply, to promote entrepreneurship and lifelong learning; and to create sustainable livelihood pathways through technical excellence.

Academic Excellence and Training Architecture:

ITI Hajin office a structured and diversified portfolio of engineering trades, designed in accordance with the standards of the national council for vocational training (NCVT) and the State Council for Vocational traning (SCVT). The institute adopts a rigorous training architecture that integrates theoretical instruction with ibtensive hands-on workshop practice. Upon successful completion of the All India Trades Test (AITT), trainees are awarded the National Trades Certificate (NTC) a nationally benchmarked credential reflecting technical proficiency, professional discipline, and employability across industrial sector.

Leadership in National Skill Ecosystems:

ITI Hajin serves as an active implementation hub for flagship national programs, most notably the PM Vishwakarma Scheme and Mission YUVA. The sucessful training and certification of the inaugural Tailor Vishwakarma cohort in December 2025 marked a significant milestone in integrating traditional craftsmanship with modern production system. Through structured training modules, advanced toolkits, and guided mentorship, the institute contributes to the revitalization of artisanal sectors while enabling decentralized economic participation and grassroots entrepreneurship.

Equipped with functional workshops and training infrastructure, ITI Hajin is steadily advancing towards a modernized, technology-enabled ecosystem. with a strategic focus on industry 4.0 readinesss, the institute is pursuing: integration of digital and blended learning platforms Development of smart workshops and upgraded training modules Strengthening of industry-institute partnerships for enhanced placement Expansion into emerging and high-demand trades.

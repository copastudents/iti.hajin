Naat Competition

Naat competitions are spiritual events where participants recite poetic praises of Prophet Muhammad (PBUH), focusing on devotion, vocal melody, and proper pronunciation. These events are popular in schools and universities to foster love for the Prophet, improve articulation, and boost confidence, often featuring prizes for top reciters.
